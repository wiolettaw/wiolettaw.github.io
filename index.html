About Me

Hi! My name is Wioletta Watroba. I am an amateur photographer based in Edinburgh, UK. Originally from Zalipie, Poland I moved to Edinburgh in 2013.

Since starting photography I have always strived to capture the essence of every moment in every photograph, whether the beauty in becalmed landscape scenary or the subtle emotions of a portrait. I hope you have enjoyed sampling my work and please feel free to contact me.
